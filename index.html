About Albania

Albania is a country in Southeast Europe, known for its beautiful coastline along the Adriatic and Ionian Seas, as well as its rugged interior of mountains and forests.

Albanian Culture

Albania has a rich and diverse cultural heritage, with influences from ancient Illyrian, Greek, Roman, Byzantine, Ottoman, and modern European cultures.

Albanian History

From ancient Illyrian tribes to modern independence, Albania has a fascinating and complex history that has shaped its culture and society.
